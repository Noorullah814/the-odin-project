Chopped Chimichurri Steak Salad

A refreshing and flavorful salad featuring tender steak and a vibrant chimichurri dressing.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb flank steak
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cups mixed salad greens
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes

Instructions

  1. Season the flank steak with salt and pepper. Grill or pan-sear to desired doneness, then let it rest before slicing thinly against the grain.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together garlic, red wine vinegar, olive oil, and crushed red pepper flakes to make the chimichurri dressing.
  3. In a large salad bowl, combine mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, red onion, parsley, and cilantro.
  4. Add the sliced steak on top of the salad and drizzle with chimichurri dressing.
  5. Toss gently to combine and serve immediately.
